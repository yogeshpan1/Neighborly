Neighborly is a Social Media platform that helps connect citizens with their local government. 
It supports online applications for Govermental Services, reportings issue, and help connect communities with News and Social feed.
The platform also enables users to report local issues such as infrastructure problems, public safety concerns, or environmental hazards, ensuring that authorities are informed quickly and can respond efficiently. Through real-time updates and tracking, citizens can stay informed about the progress of their requests and reports.
In addition, Neighborly fosters stronger communities by offering a social feed where users can share news, updates, and important announcements. This helps keep residents engaged, aware of local developments, and connected with one another. By combining civic services with social interaction, Neighborly creates a more transparent, responsive, and community-driven local governance experience.
